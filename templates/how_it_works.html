The Technology Behind RankCV

TF-IDF Vectorization

Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency converts text into meaningful numerical vectors, highlighting important terms while filtering out common words.

Cosine Similarity

Measures the cosine of the angle between two vectors, determining how closely a candidate's CV matches the job description regardless of document length.

Secure Processing

All CVs are processed in-memory and never stored permanently unless you explicitly choose to save them. Enterprise-grade encryption protects your data.

Real-time Analysis

Get results in seconds, not hours. Process multiple CVs simultaneously with our optimized Python backend powered by scikit-learn.
