How It Works

01

The recruiter pastes a job description and uploads CVs as PDFs

02

RankCV extracts text from the PDFs and analyzes the meaning behind the words — not just matching keywords

03

Candidates are ranked by relevance from 0% to 100%. The best fits appear at the top, saving hours of manual screening

How It Understands a CV

It Knows Synonyms

Unlike basic keyword matching, RankCV uses Sentence-BERT — a neural model that understands meaning.

Job: "Python developer"
CV: "Python programmer"

Traditional matching would score this low. RankCV scores it high because it grasps they mean the same thing.

It Reads in Chunks

Long CVs are broken into small sections. The system finds the most relevant part of each CV and compares that to the job.

A 10-page CV won't automatically win just because it's longer. A short 2-page CV with the right experience can rank higher — because only the best-matching section counts.

Powered by Sentence-BERT (all-MiniLM-L6-v2) — the same technology behind modern search engines
