How It Works

01

The recruiter pastes a job description and uploads CVs as PDFs

02

RankCV reads the PDFs and understands what each CV actually says — not just matching words, but grasping the meaning

03

Candidates are ranked by relevance from 0% to 100%. The best fits appear at the top, saving hours of manual screening

How the AI "Understands" a CV

It Knows Synonyms

Unlike simple keyword matching, RankCV knows that "Python developer" and "Python programmer" mean the same thing.

If the job asks for "machine learning," a CV saying "deep learning" or "AI model building" will still score highly because the AI grasps the connection.

It Reads in Chunks

Long CVs are broken into small sections. The AI finds the most relevant part of each CV and compares that to the job.

A 10-page CV won't automatically win just because it's longer. A short 2-page CV with the right experience can rank higher.

Think of it like Google Search — but for hiring
