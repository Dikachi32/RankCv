RankCV

AI-Powered CV Ranking System

Built with Flask by Dikachi

How It Works

01

User enters a job description and uploads CVs (PDF)

02

System extracts text using PyPDF2 and vectorizes with TF-IDF

03

Ranks candidates by cosine similarity score (0-100%)

TF-IDF Explained

TF

Term Frequency checks how many times a word appears

If "Python" appears many times, it is important

IDF

Inverse Document Frequency removes common words

Words like "the" and "is" are not important

We use scikit-learn to turn text into numbers

Ranking System

1
Best Match
2
Good Match
3
Low Match

More similar words → Higher Score
Less similar → Lower Score
